Corporate Overview

A Swiss-led group delivering curated European experiences

Alpii Group is a Switzerland-based travel company operating as both a tour operator and a curated marketplace for premium European experiences.
The Group designs, manages, and delivers high-quality experiences across Europe through a controlled network of selected local partners. All operations are governed by Swiss-led standards of quality, reliability, compliance, and operational discipline.
Alpii’s role extends beyond distribution. The Group maintains direct oversight of experience design, partner selection, service execution, and ongoing quality assurance.

Operating Model

Centralized governance with localized execution

Alpii operates under an integrated group structure that combines centralized strategic governance with localized operational execution.
Strategic leadership, quality frameworks, and governance are defined at Group level, ensuring consistency across markets. Local operations are executed through vetted partners who operate in alignment with Alpii’s service standards and performance requirements.
This model enables Alpii to scale across Europe while maintaining a controlled and consistent level of service delivery.

Our Philosophy

The Alpii Standard

The Alpii Standard defines how experiences are designed, delivered, and continuously refined across our network.
It reflects a Swiss-led commitment to quality, accountability, and long-term partnership.
